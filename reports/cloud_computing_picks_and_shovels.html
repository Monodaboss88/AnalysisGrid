Investment Thesis

The Premise

Every dollar of Cloud / SaaS Infrastructure's revenue flows through a small set of critical suppliers. These "picks & shovels" companies have monopoly or oligopoly moats, sell to multiple end customers simultaneously, and benefit regardless of which end customer wins.

Why It Works

During the Gold Rush, the most reliable profits went to those selling pickaxes ‚Äî not to miners. The infrastructure layer has higher margins, stickier revenue, and less competitive risk than the application layer.
