Investment Thesis

The Premise

Every dollar of AI revenue from NVDA, MSFT, AMZN, GOOGL, and META flows through a small set of critical suppliers. These "picks & shovels" companies have monopoly or oligopoly moats, sell to multiple hyperscalers simultaneously, and benefit regardless of which AI company wins.

Why It Works

During the Gold Rush, the most reliable profits went to those selling pickaxes and shovels ‚Äî not to miners. Today's AI gold rush is no different. The infrastructure layer has higher margins, stickier revenue, and less competitive risk than the application layer.
