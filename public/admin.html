AI Features Enabled
When disabled, the Trade Desk hides the AI toggle and all AI analysis features. Scanners fall back to deterministic rules only.
⚠️ Data Transparency
When AI is enabled, the following data may be sent to AI providers (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI GPT):
• Ticker symbols and price data from public market feeds
• Scanner output (technical indicators, regime classifications)
• User-written trade notes (for AI commentary)
Never sent: PnL figures, account balances, position sizes, personal information, login credentials.
AI providers do not use this data for training per their enterprise API agreements.
